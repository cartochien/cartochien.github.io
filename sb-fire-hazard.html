Santa Barbara Fire Hazard Severity Map

This was a project I did on my own time to learn how to use Leaflet JS. It is still a work in progress, but it currently features the boundaries of what are considered to be moderate, high, or very high risk State Responsibility Areas in Santa Barbara County. It also includes the location and contact information of every fire station in the county. Click the link below to see the map. Tips and guidance on Leaflet and interactive webmapping are highly appreciated...
