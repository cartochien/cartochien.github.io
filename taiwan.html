Taiwan Shaded Relief

This was a project I did on my own time to learn how to make more elegant shaded relief maps. I made this map by preparing my own DEM and then using Blender to create a three dimensional model of it. I then combined it with landcover data in Adobe Photoshop. In the future, I plan on coloring the map to make it more realistic and then finish it off with labeling of major cities and landmarks.
